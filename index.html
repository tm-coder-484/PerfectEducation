About us

Help that actually gets you

Perfect Education is a student-led tutoring club. The tutors are older students who recently sat the same classes you're in — so they explain things the way you'd want a mate to.

1

Apply online

Tell us your name, year, the subjects you want help with, and when you're free. Takes two minutes.

2

Get matched

We pair you with a tutor who's strong in that subject and email you a session time that fits your week.

3

Show up & learn

Bring your homework, a question, or just your confusion. 30 minutes later you'll get it. Come back anytime.
